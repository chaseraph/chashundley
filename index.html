About Me

I'm Chas Hundley, a journalist, editor, and publisher based in Oregon.

I founded and run three community-driven news outlets — News in the Grove, the Banks Post, and the Gales Creek Journal — and one digital special-interest magazine for news in and around the Tillamook Forest: Salmonberry Magazine.

I am a firm believer in joyful innovation in the local news publishing space, and am constantly trying to find new ways to bring high-quality, kickass journalism to local people.

I believe in shoe-leather reporting, civic storytelling, and the quiet power of the independent press.
